Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

Lifeline Connect (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is developed and operated by Health Impact Alliance. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal information when you use the Lifeline Connect mobile application (“App”).

Lifeline Connect is designed to support caregivers — including family members and professional caregivers — in monitoring and assisting older adults through connected devices and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

Users create accounts through our website and log into the App using those credentials. We may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (stored securely and encrypted)

Authentication is managed through our secure backend systems.

Device and Usage Data

We collect information related to:

  • Device usage patterns
  • App interaction data
  • Connected ecosystem device activity
  • Performance metrics and diagnostics

This information helps caregivers understand how supported devices are being used and how they contribute to wellbeing.

Location Data (Optional)

If enabled by the user and supported by connected devices, we may collect location data. Location tracking is optional and controlled by user consent. Users may disable location permissions at any time through device settings.

Camera and Media Usage

The App may request camera access to:

  • Support video calling functionality
  • Allow users to capture and send images within chat features

Camera access occurs only when the user actively initiates camera functionality. We do not access the camera in the background.

Communications Data

We may process:

  • Messages sent within chat features
  • Images shared within the App
  • Video call session data (transmitted securely)

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide caregiver monitoring services
  • Enable communication features (chat and video calls)
  • Analyze device interaction trends
  • Improve product functionality and performance
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

3. Data Storage and Security

All collected data is stored on secure servers operated by Health Impact Alliance. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data, including encrypted transmission and secure authentication systems.

4. Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Authorized caregiving partners
  • Healthcare providers
  • Service providers supporting our infrastructure

We share information only when necessary to provide services or when authorized by the user. We do not sell personal data.

5. Analytics

We may implement analytics tools to improve functionality and performance. Any analytics implementation will comply with applicable privacy regulations and will be reflected in updates to this Privacy Policy.

6. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access to your data, correction, deletion, or withdrawal of consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at:

Email: support@healthimpactalliance.com

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and support caregiving functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Lifeline Connect is intended for general audiences and caregivers. The App is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
